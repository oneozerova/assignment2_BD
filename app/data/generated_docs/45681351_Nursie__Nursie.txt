Nursie! Nursie! is a 1916 British silent comedy film directed by Alexander Butler and starring James W. Tate, Clarice Mayne and Bernard Vaughan. It was made at Isleworth Studios.Harris p.201 ==Cast== * James W. Tate as Patient * Clarice Mayne as Nurse * Bernard Vaughan as Doctor * Percy C.
