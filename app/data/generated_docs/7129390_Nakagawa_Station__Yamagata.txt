is a railway station on the Ōu Main Line in the city of Nanyō, Yamagata Prefecture, Japan, operated by East Japan Railway Company (JR East). ==Lines== Nakagawa Station is served by the Ōu Main Line, and is located 64.4 rail kilometers from the terminus of the line at Fukushima Station. ==Station layout== Nakagawa Station has two opposed side platforms connected via a pedestrian overpass; however, only one platform is in use. The station is unattended. ===Platforms=== ==History== Nakagawa Station opened on November 3, 1903.
