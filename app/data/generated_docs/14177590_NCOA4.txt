Nuclear receptor coactivator 4, also known as Androgen Receptor Activator (ARA70), is a protein that in humans is encoded by the NCOA4 gene. It plays an important role in ferritinophagy, acting as a cargo receptor, binding to the ferritin heavy chain and latching on to ATG8 on the surface of the autophagosome. == Interactions == NCOA4 has been shown to interact with: * Androgen receptor, and * Peroxisome proliferator-activated receptor gamma * Ferritin * ATG8 == See also == * Transcription coregulator == References == == Further reading == * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * == External links == * Category:Gene expression Category:Transcription coregulators
