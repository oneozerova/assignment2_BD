Nipponaphera goniata is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Cancellariidae, the nutmeg snails. ==Description== The length of the shell attains 16.1 mm. ==Distribution== This marine species occurs off New Caledonia. ==References== ==External links== * Bouchet P. & Petit R.E.
