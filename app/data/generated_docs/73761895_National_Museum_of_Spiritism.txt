Museu Nacional do Espiritismo located in Curitiba, in the Brazilian state of Paraná, is a museum on subjects related to the Spiritist Doctrine. The MUNESPI collection contains mediumistic objects such as photographs, recordings on magnetic tapes, psychopictographs, materializations and psychographs.
